# <span style="color: #5e6970;">Chicago-based HubTran Taps J.P. Lauz for Customer Success Role</span> {.h1-case-study-title}

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\][vc\_column\_text]**Chicago-based HubTran Taps J.P. Lauz for Customer Success Role**

Chicago, December 17, 2019 — After gaining critical international experience at Citibank and earning his MBA from The University of Chicago’s Booth School of Business, J.P. Lauz has joined HubTran, creators of invoice processing automation for the transportation industry. As Director of Customer Success, Lauz onboards new customers and continually helps them improve effectiveness and reduce costs.

&nbsp;

Matt Bernstein, CEO of HubTran, said of Lauz, “Delivering great results means excellent preparation and training. Because each customer has unique needs, there’s no such thing as cookie-cutter training. In fact, we work hard to learn something new and improve our product with each new customer. JP’s computer engineering degree from Notre Dame, experience implementing tech products at Citi, and his MBA from Chicago Booth make him a great fit.”

&nbsp;

Lauz said, “I am excited to be working at HubTran. It’s inspiring to join innovators who’ve built a product that fills a critical need in the logistics space. HubTran’s dynamic work environment, cutting-edge technology, and world-class team made the decision easy. HubTran’s enthusiastic customers make the job a dream.”

&nbsp;

Josh Asbury, VP of HubTran, on Lauz: “Brokers and factors love HubTran, and we enjoy a statistical 100% renewal rate. JP is here to make the user experience even better.”

&nbsp;
