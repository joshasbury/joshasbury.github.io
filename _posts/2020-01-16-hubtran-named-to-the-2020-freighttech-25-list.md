# <span style="color: #5e6970;">HubTran Makes Freightwaves&#8217;s Top 25 Innovative Companies List</span> {.h1-case-study-title}

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\][vc\_column\_text]**Chicago, February 13, 2020** — On November 13 at the FreightWaves LIVE conference in Chicago, an external panel of industry experts ranked a list of 100 customer-nominated freight companies and winnowed that list to the 25 “most innovative and disruptive companies across the freight industry.” Along with some of world’s biggest and brightest, HubTran—creators of invoice processing automation—was named to the FreightTech 25.

In their article spotlighting HubTran, FreightWaves described the company as “at the forefront of the digital push for automating the back office.” (<https://www.freightwaves.com/news/hubtran-earns-spot-on-2020-freighttech-25-list>)

&nbsp;

HubTran customer, Jason Doris, CTO of AM Transport Services, congratulated the 4-year-old company. “We absolutely love HubTran. It does the hard work for us when processing carrier invoices. The machine learning used to read the documents and sync with our TMS is seamless. Processing invoices and imaging documents is much faster and more accurate. We now have more time to focus on more important aspects of the business.”

&nbsp;

Matt Bernstein, HubTran CEO, on the honor: “We’re thrilled to be on FreightTech 25 list. That pride begins with having incredible customers who nominated us and put us in the top 100. A big thanks to them, and also to the professionals who judged us to be worthy of the short list. ”<span class="Apple-converted-space"><br /> </span>

&nbsp;

The list features both cutting-edge startups and traditional powerhouses. While HubTran made the top 100 list in 2019, this is the company’s first time on the FreightTech25.<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\][vc\_column\_text]**About HubTran:**<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>

HubTran has sparked a back-office revolution by replacing old-school systems with automation to efficiently process invoices, bill customers, and manage documents. The company does it with enterprise-grade software using optical character recognition and artificial intelligence that’s smart, easy, and affordable. HubTran 3PLs and factors work 4x faster with better cash flow and incredible accuracy.

&nbsp;

HubTran recently earned a spot on FreightWaves’ Top 25 most innovative companies list. Other awards include the Harvard Business School New Venture Competition, Chicago Innovation Award, and Executives’ Club of Chicago Innovator of the Year.
