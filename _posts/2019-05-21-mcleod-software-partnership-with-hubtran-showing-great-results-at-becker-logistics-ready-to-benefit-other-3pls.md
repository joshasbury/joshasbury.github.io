# <span style="color: #5e6970;">McLeod Software Partnership with HubTran Showing Great Results for Becker Logistics, Ready to Benefit Other 3PLs</span> {.h1-case-study-title}

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\][vc\_column\_text]McLeod Software, a supplier of transportation management and trucking software for carriers, brokers, third-party logistics providers (3PLs), and shippers announces its partnership with HubTran, the leading back-office automation for transportation companies.

&nbsp;

The partnership allows McLeod’s PowerBroker® customers to easily and affordably replace obsolete, back-office processes with speedy HubTran automation. HubTran uses optical character recognition and artificial intelligence to automate carrier payables and invoicing by reading, auditing, and organizing transportation documents.

&nbsp;

“Working with Matt Bernstein and his team has allowed us to build a solution to improve the back office processing of carrier payment auditing, and in turn speed the invoicing and cash collection from their customers. Improved efficiency in the back office allows our PowerBroker customers to grow while keeping overhead in check,” said Robert Brothers, Director of Product Development for McLeod Software.

&nbsp;

Matt Bernstein, CEO of HubTran, explains how the partnership evolved, “Becker Logistics, one of the fastest growing freight brokers in the U.S., pushed hard for this because they wanted an easy-to-use, low-cost system that works in lockstep with PowerBroker and DocumentPower™. McLeod Software is one of the most respected technology companies in transportation, and we’re delighted that we can now offer their customers the industry’s leading back-office automation solution.”

&nbsp;

Jim Becker, CEO of Becker Logistics, commented on the HubTran experience, “We have found that when our carriers properly email their invoices and bills of lading, productivity has increased four times. And when our carriers do not, HubTran helps us respond in a more efficient, organized way. Using HubTran allows us to continue our fast-paced growth track without having to add additional people on our accounting team. Matt Bernstein and HubTran, along with McLeod, have been fantastic during the 4-week transition. We would like to thank and acknowledge them for a fantastic package.”

&nbsp;

Bernstein adds, “The HubTran integration with McLeod Software is rock solid and will enable McLeod customers to instantly improve their businesses. HubTran is industrial-strength, high-tech software that really works for 3PLs. It’s paperless, mistake-proof, and reduces costs by 60%.”\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\]\[vc\_separator type=&#8221;normal&#8221; color=&#8221;#e9933b&#8221; thickness=&#8221;2px&#8221;\]\[vc\_column_text\]**About McLeod Software:**<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>

<p id="953e" class="hd he cn ar hf b hg hh hi hj hk hl hm hn ho hp hq dv" data-selectable-paragraph="">
  McLeod Software is the leading provider of transportation and logistics solutions, including business intelligence, accounting, customer relationship management, pricing, quoting, rating, dispatch operations, EDI and order entry, fleet management, driver planning, HOS management, business process automation with visual workflow, and document management, all from one company in one integrated system. To learn more, visit <a class="bo dd sy sz ta tb" href="http://www.mcleodsoftware.com/" target="_blank" rel="noopener nofollow noreferrer">www.McLeodSoftware.com</a>.
</p>

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space\][vc\_column\_text]**About HubTran:**<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>

HubTran has sparked a back-office revolution by replacing old-school systems with automation to efficiently process invoices, bill customers, and manage documents. The company does it with enterprise-grade software using optical character recognition and artificial intelligence that’s smart, easy, and affordable. HubTran 3PLs and factors work 4x faster with better cash flow and incredible accuracy.

HubTran recently earned a spot on FreightWaves’ Top 25 most innovative companies list. Other awards include the Harvard Business School New Venture Competition, Chicago Innovation Award, and Executives’ Club of Chicago Innovator of the Year. The most important reviews come from HubTran customers who can’t say enough about product performance, minimal startup time, and excellent customer service.\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space\][vc\_column\_text]**About Becker Logistics, LLC:**<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>

Becker Logistics is a transportation management company that matches customers with top-quality freight carriers in the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Becker has a large, dependable network of quality truck and rail carriers.
