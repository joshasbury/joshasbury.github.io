# <span style="color: #5e6970;">Logistics Providers Embrace HubTran Back-Office Automation</span> {.h1-case-study-title}

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\][vc\_column\_text]Chicago IL — November 15, 2016 — HubTran today released the names of 11 third-party logistics providers who have recently implemented HubTran back-office automation. They join the many brokers and factors who have adopted HubTran since its official launch just one year ago.

&nbsp;

Cloud-based HubTran offers exceptional back-office automation that leapfrogs traditional document management with sophisticated optical character recognition, artificial intelligence, and intuitive workflows.

&nbsp;

“Adoption of HubTran is growing exponentially,” said CEO Matt Bernstein. “We’re excited at the level of enthusiasm the industry has shown over this past year.”

&nbsp;

Bernstein said the most recent companies to implement HubTran range from mid-level domestic brokers to international 3PLs. They are:

Dupré Logistics, Lafayette LA

Evans Transportation Services, Brookfield WI

Lynnco Supply Chain Solutions, Tulsa OK

NAL Native American Logistics, Flint MI

Odyssey Logistics & Technology, Danbury CT

One Source, Louisville KY<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>

Priority1, Little Rock, AR

R2 Freight and Logistics, Indianapolis IN

RR Donnelley, Chicago IL

TOP Worldwide, Flint MI

WOW Logistics, Appleton WI.

&nbsp;

John Redam, Jr., Vendor Relations Manager at Priority1, a 21-year old 3PL managing over 500,000 North American shipments annually said, “HubTran has helped us streamline our back office process. HubTran is allowing Priority1 to continue to focus on growth and customer acquisition.”

&nbsp;

Kelly Navin, Manager of Transportation Services at WOW Logistics, a nationally recognized leader in the third-party logistics industry, said, “Within two weeks HubTran helped us cut the amount of time we spend billing and matching carrier invoices to loads by more than half.”\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space\][vc\_column\_text]**About HubTran:**<span class="Apple-converted-space"> </span>

HubTran has sparked a back-office revolution by replacing old-school systems with automation to efficiently process invoices, bill customers, and manage documents. The company does it with enterprise-grade software using optical character recognition and artificial intelligence that’s smart, easy, and affordable. HubTran 3PLs and factors work 4x faster with better cash flow and incredible accuracy.

HubTran recently earned a spot on FreightWaves’ Top 25 most innovative companies list. Other awards include the Harvard Business School New Venture Competition, Chicago Innovation Award, and Executives’ Club of Chicago Innovator of the Year.
