Tinamarie Sulpizio to Join HubTran as Director Business Development

Chicago (July 6, 2017) HubTran, the leading back-office automation technology used by transportation and factoring companies announced that Tinamarie Sulpizio will join HubTran as Director Business Development effective July 17, 2017.

Tinamarie brings significant experience serving brokers, 3PLs and factoring companies, most recently as Director of Sales at Ansonia Credit Data. Through her leadership positions in both sales and operations, Tinamarie has developed deep knowledge and trusted relationships throughout the industry.
As Director Business Development, Tinamarie will help HubTran accelerate its growth and reinforce its commitment to providing high quality service to their transportation and factoring customers.

Tinamarie commented, Im excited to bring HubTrans amazing automation platform to more factors and 3PLs. Throughout my career Ive always worked to help my customers grow and protect their businesses. I see HubTran as a perfect fit for my customers that will make their businesses even better.
Matt Bernstein, CEO of HubTran remarked, Tinamaries track record of success, innovation and commitment to doing right by customers make her a great addition to our team.

About HubTran:

HubTran has sparked a back-office revolution by replacing old-school systems with automation to efficiently process invoices, bill customers, and manage documents.The company does it with enterprise-grade software using optical character recognition and artificial intelligence thats smart, easy, and affordable. HubTran 3PLs and factors work 4x faster with better cash flow and incredible accuracy.

HubTran recently earned a spot on FreightWaves Top 25 most innovative companies list. Other awards include the Harvard Business School New Venture Competition, Chicago Innovation Award, and Executives Club of Chicago Innovator of the Year. The most important reviews come from HubTran customers who cant say enough about product performance, minimal startup time, and excellent customer service.
