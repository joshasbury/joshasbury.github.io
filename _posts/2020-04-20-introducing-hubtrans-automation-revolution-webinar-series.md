# <span style="color: #5e6970;">HubTran&#8217;s Automation Revolution Webinar Series</span> {.h1-case-study-title}

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\]\[vc\_single\_image image=&#8221;3251&#8243; img\_size=&#8221;large&#8221; alignment=&#8221;center&#8221; onclick=&#8221;custom\_link&#8221; qode\_css\_animation=&#8221;&#8221; link=&#8221;https://zoom.us/webinar/register/5015867871031/WN\_uYBcH7BpQum6rKzBOI-YVQ&#8221;\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\][vc\_column_text]Since nearly everyone is working from home these days, we are working on a series of webinars intended to help our 3PL, Broker, Factor and Forwarder customers gain immense efficiencies in the transportation back-office.

&nbsp;

The first in our series is an overview of HubTran&#8217;s solution for <a href="https://hubtran.wpmudev.host/3pls/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">3PLs and Brokers</a> and will occur on **April 24, 2020** at **1:00 EDT**. We will do a deep-dive into <a href="https://hubtran.wpmudev.host/3pls/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">HubTran&#8217;s Carrier Invoicing and Customer Billing</a> solutions with the intent of showing you how HubTran&#8217;s machine learning and artificial intelligence can greatly improve your back-office&#8217;s efficiency and accuracy.

&nbsp;

<a href="https://zoom.us/webinar/register/5015867871031/WN_uYBcH7BpQum6rKzBOI-YVQ" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"><strong>Click here to register.</strong></a>

&nbsp;

Next week, we will broadcast a joint webinar with our partners at <a href="https://www.mcleodsoftware.com/3pl-brokerage/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">McLeod Software</a> where we speak to best practices in security in this new work-from-home environment.

&nbsp;
