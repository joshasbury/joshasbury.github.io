# <span style="color: #5e6970;">HubTran Names Brian Rudich COO</span> {.h1-case-study-title}

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\][vc\_column\_text]Chicago IL &#8212; August 1, 2016 &#8212; Matt Bernstein, CEO of HubTran, has named Brian Rudich HubTran&#8217;s Chief Operating Officer. According to Bernstein, Rudich will oversee HubTran&#8217;s growth as the provider of choice for advanced transportation back-office automation software.

&nbsp;

A cloud-based platform, HubTran leapfrogs traditional document management with sophisticated optical character recognition, artificial intelligence, and intuitive workflows.

&nbsp;

Before joining HubTran, Rudich served as Chief Financial Officer for Command Transportation, which launched in 2005. As the company&#8217;s fourth employee, Rudich nurtured Command&#8217;s technology resources, contributing to steady 60% growth, annual revenues of $580 million, and a place among Transport Topics&#8217; top 25 freight brokers. Command was purchased for $420 million last year by Echo Global Logistics. The resulting company reports revenues of more than $1.5 billion.

&nbsp;

&#8220;Throughout his career, Brian has focused on using technology to create back-office efficiencies. His deep experience and notable success at Command make him the ideal choice for COO at HubTran,&#8221; said Bernstein.

&nbsp;
