# <span style="color: #5e6970;">MercuryGate Extends Invoicing and Document Management Solution with HubTran Integration</span> {.h1-case-study-title}

\[/vc\_column\_text\]\[vc\_empty\_space height=&#8221;30px&#8221;\][vc\_column\_text]<article> <section class="dv dw dx dy dz"> 

<div class="n p">
  <div class="z ab ac ae af ea ah ai">
    <p>
      CARY, N.C.&#8211;(<a href="https://www.businesswire.com/">BUSINESS WIRE</a>)&#8211;MercuryGate International, Inc., the industry-leading provider of transportation management software, today announced its partnership with HubTran, a cloud based provider of back-office automation and document management for transportation companies.
    </p>
    
    <p>
      &nbsp;
    </p>
    
    <p>
      Shippers and logistics service providers often deal with inefficient and outdated invoice processing systems leading to labor intensive and error prone invoice management. In today’s competitive environment, maximizing back office productivity and ensuring that carriers are paid on time and accurately can greatly increase a company’s competitive advantage and ability to secure capacity.
    </p>
    
    <p>
      &nbsp;
    </p>
    
    <p>
      MercuryGate is fully integrated with HubTran, which can recognize, sort and organize transportation documents such as invoices, bills of lading, proof of delivery and other supporting documents. HubTran uses advanced machine learning algorithms and optical character recognition to discern and extract critical information from these documents.
    </p>
    
    <p>
      &nbsp;
    </p>
    
    <p>
      “MercuryGate clients today benefit from a fully automated freight invoice management solution that provides cost allocations, accruals, audits and settlements. MercuryGate’s integration with HubTran extends this automation and collaborative workflow even further,” said Monica Wooden, MercuryGate CEO and Co-Founder.
    </p>
    
    <p>
      &nbsp;
    </p>
    
    <p>
      “HubTran and MercuryGate are committed to delivering technology solutions that help transportation companies drive efficiency and growth. This integration provides a seamless platform for companies that want to increase their back office capacity and accuracy, while significantly reducing cost. We’re very pleased to partner with MercuryGate,” said Matt Bernstein, HubTran CEO.
    </p>
    
    <p>
      &nbsp;
    </p>
    
    <p>
      Access to HubTran document management is available now to all MercuryGate clients who can leverage these capabilities seamlessly embedded into the MercuryGate solution.
    </p>
    
    <p>
      &nbsp;
    </p>
    
    <p>
      For more information about the MercuryGate and HubTran partnership, contact us directly at: <a href="mailto:sales@mercurygate.com">sales@mercurygate.com</a>.
    </p>
    
    <p>
      &nbsp;
    </p>
    
    <p>
      <b>About MercuryGate International, Inc.</b>
    </p>
    
    <p>
      &nbsp;
    </p>
    
    <p>
      MercuryGate International delivers collaborative, global transportation management solutions for shippers, carriers, brokers, freight forwarders and third party logistics providers enabling them to plan, optimize, execute and settle global transportation movements while providing complete end-to-end control tower visibility and decision support. With MercuryGate’s configurable architecture and workflow engine, MercuryGate clients are able to manage parcel, LTL, intermodal and truckload shipments as well as complex multi-leg, ocean, air, and rail movements. Visit MercuryGate at: <a href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.mercurygate.com&esheet=51241672&newsitemid=20151210006370&lan=en-US&anchor=www.mercurygate.com&index=1&md5=ea72d95edcd046d4058ae7cefe708ab7">www.mercurygate.com</a> or contact us directly at: <a href="mailto:sales@mercurygate.com">sales@mercurygate.com</a>.
    </p>
  </div>
